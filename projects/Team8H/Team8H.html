Eight Hour Team Game

Controls at the Bottom


Link to Repo

This project was a mini game jam idea our instructor had. We were allowed to pick our partners and were set off to create a simple game in only eight hours. Me and my partner decided to build a simple platforming game and this is the result we have come up with.


Controls:

W or Up Arrow to Jump
A or Left Arrow to Move Left
D or Right Arrow to Move Right
